Where It All Began

We were lucky enough to meet in March 2021 through a mutual friend. Harry had known this friend from school and kept in touch, Hannah had met this friend whilst she was in sixth form. Luckily enough she suggested setting us up for a little date. One date later and we have been inseparable ever since. We thank our lucky stars and our friend every day for bringing us together.

When We Booked Our First Trip

We booked our very first trip to Iceland back in July 2021 for January 2022. That following month we booked 3 more trips - which then gave us the inspiration of travel. Especially after covid as our trips planned for the end of 2021 sadly got cancelled which just made our travel bug worse.
